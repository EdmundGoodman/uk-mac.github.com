Mantevo 3.0 Release - 19th November 2014

At SC14 New Orleans version 3.0 of the Mantevo suite was released.

This release contained four codes from the UK-MAC collection: CloverLeaf V1.1, CloverLeaf3D V1.0, CleverLeaf V2.0 and TeaLeaf V1.0.

The suite represents a co-design effort across a number of US national laboratories, and is an essential tool for evaluating new HPC hardware and programming models.
